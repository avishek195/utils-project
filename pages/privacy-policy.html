Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

At ToolWave, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and tools.

Information We Collect

Files and Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit your files or document contents. All file and text processing happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your PDFs, resumes, and other inputs never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Because processing is performed locally, we do not have access to the documents or text you use with our tools, and we do not create backups or logs of your file contents.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain limited technical information:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses

This information is collected through standard web server logs and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar services) to help us understand how visitors use our website and improve our services. This data is aggregated and is not linked to the specific contents of your files.

How We Use Information

We use automatically collected information to:

  • Improve our website and tools
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Ensure website security
  • Provide better user experience

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements (if applicable)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Services and Advertising

We may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Analytics: We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand website usage and improve performance.
  • CDN Services: We use content delivery networks to serve our website assets faster and more reliably.
  • Advertising (Including Google AdSense): We may display advertisements through third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense. These providers may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads and measure performance.

These third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal data or the contents of your files to any third party. Any advertising or analytics usage is focused on anonymous usage patterns, not on the specific documents you process with our tools.

Google AdSense and Cookies

Google, as a third-party vendor, may use cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or by using other industry opt-out mechanisms where available in your region.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your files, there is no risk of your document contents being compromised on our servers. However, we implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect our website and any limited information we do collect (such as logs or analytics data) from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
