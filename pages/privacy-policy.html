Introduction

At ToolWave, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and tools.

Information We Collect

Files and Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit your files or data. All file processing happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information:

  • IP address

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages

This information is collected through standard web server logs and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to help us understand how visitors use our website and improve our services.

How We Use Information

We use automatically collected information to:

  • Improve our website and tools

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Analyze website traffic
  • Provide personalized content (if applicable)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

These third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your files, there's no risk of your data being compromised on our servers. However, we implement appropriate security measures to protect our website and any information we do collect.
