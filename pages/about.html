About ToolWave

Our Mission

ToolWave was created with a simple mission: to provide free, high-quality utility tools that help people work more efficiently. We believe that essential tools should be accessible to everyone, without barriers, subscriptions, or privacy concerns.

What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive suite of online utility tools organized into three main categories:

PDF Tools

Our PDF tools help you compress, merge, and convert PDF files. All processing happens in your browser, ensuring your documents never leave your device.

Resume Tools

Our resume optimization tools help job seekers create ATS-friendly resumes. Check keywords, format your resume, and improve your chances of getting noticed by employers.

Developer Tools

Essential tools for developers including JSON formatting and JWT decoding. Fast, reliable, and completely free.

Our Values

  • Privacy First: All processing happens locally in your browser. We never see, store, or access your files.
  • Free Forever: All our tools are completely free with no hidden fees or subscriptions.
  • No Sign-ups: Use our tools immediately without creating an account.
  • Quality: We strive to provide professional-grade tools that deliver reliable results.
  • Accessibility: Our tools work on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile.

How We Work

Unlike many online tools, ToolWave processes everything in your browser using client-side JavaScript. This means:

  • Your files never leave your device
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • Complete privacy and security
  • Fast processing without upload delays

Technology

ToolWave is built using modern web technologies:

  • HTML5 and CSS3 for structure and styling
  • Vanilla JavaScript for functionality
  • PDF.js and PDF-lib for PDF processing
  • Client-side processing for privacy
